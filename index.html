The Problem

Womens health research is flying blind.

Women's health research is chronically underfunded, built on male-biased data, and fragmented across disciplines that rarely speak to each other. Conditions like endometriosis, PCOS, adenomyosis, and early gynaecological cancers affect hundreds of millions of women — yet produce few disease-modifying treatments and suffer decade-long diagnostic delays.

The evidence exists. It's just scattered, inconsistent, and nearly impossible to navigate without spending months doing it manually.

01 PCOS prevalence in reproductive age women 1 in 10
02 Ovarian cancers caught at advanced stage ~75%

The Platform

From question to decision, faster.

Nexagen ingests the full women's health literature — starting with endometriosis: studies, trials, patents, and your own lab data. It returns ranked, traceable recommendations. No bioinformatics expertise required.

01
Ingest & Structure
Gathers published studies, clinical trials, patents, and internal lab data. Extracts structured variables relevant to women's health: hormone status, disease stage, sample type, and assay type.
02
Score & Rank
Ranks biomarkers, drug targets, and study designs by evidence quality. Built on disease-specific ontologies — starting with endometriosis — so you can compare messy studies apples-to-apples across conditions.
03
Decide with Confidence
Every recommendation links directly to its source papers, trials, and datasets. Auditable by scientists, funders, and regulators. Exportable for grant applications.

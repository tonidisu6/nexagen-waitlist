The Problem

Endometriosis research is flying blind.

Endometriosis is a chronic, systemic inflammatory disease affecting 190 million women worldwide. Despite its prevalence, research remains fragmented across immunology, gynaecology, and genomics: built on male-biased data, underfunded relative to its burden, and producing no disease-modifying treatments in decades.

The evidence exists. It's just scattered, inconsistent, and nearly impossible to navigate without spending months doing it manually.

The Platform

From question to decision, faster.

Nexagen ingests the full endometriosis literature: studies, trials, patents, and your own lab data. It returns ranked, traceable recommendations. No bioinformatics expertise required.

01
Ingest & Structure
Gathers published studies, clinical trials, patents, and internal lab data. Extracts structured variables: hormone status, disease stage, sample type, and assay type.
02
Score & Rank
Ranks biomarkers, drug targets, and study designs by evidence quality. Built on endometriosis-specific variables so you can compare messy studies apples-to-apples.
03
Decide with Confidence
Every recommendation links directly to its source papers, trials, and datasets. Auditable by scientists, funders, and regulators. Exportable for grant applications.
